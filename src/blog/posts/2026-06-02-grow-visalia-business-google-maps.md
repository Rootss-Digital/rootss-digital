Do you want more people to buy from your shop in Visalia? When people look for a place to eat or buy things, they use their phones. They search for things "near me."

If your business is not on the map, people will not see it. They will go to a big chain store on Mooney Boulevard instead of your local shop. Let's learn how to fix that so more neighbors can find you.

## Why Google Maps Matters for Visalia Shops

Google Maps is like a giant, digital phone book — but much smarter. When someone types in "bakery" or "plumber," Google looks for the closest shop.

If you are at the top of the map, good things happen:

- More people see your business name
- More people call your phone number
- More people drive to your store

## Step 1: Fix Your Free Google Profile

Google lets you make a free page for your business. It is like an online business card. You must make sure all the facts on it are correct.

### Use the Exact Same Name and Phone Number

Make sure your name, street address, and phone number are the same everywhere. If your shop is on Main Street, write it the same way on Google, Facebook, and your website. If you change it, Google gets confused.

### Add Pictures of Your Real Shop in Visalia

Take nice pictures with your phone and put them on your Google page. You can take pictures of the front of your store, your friendly workers, and the things you sell. People love to see real pictures of Visalia before they visit.

## Step 2: Use Words That Tell People Where You Are

When you write text for your website, do not just say you are in "California." Use words that show you are a real part of our town.

Use local words that people know, like:

- *"Down the street from the Visalia Fox Theatre."*
- *"Serving families near the College of the Sequoias."*
- *"Located right in Downtown Visalia."*

When you use these words, Google learns exactly where you are — and shows your shop to people who live nearby.

## Step 3: Ask Your Customers for Stars

When happy customers leave five stars on Google, it helps you win. Google likes shops that have lots of good reviews and shows those shops first.

**Easy tip:** When a customer is paying at the register, say: *"Thank you for shopping local! Would you please leave us a quick review on Google tonight?"*

The more great reviews you get from people in Tulare County, the higher your business will climb on the map.

## Start Growing Your Visalia Business Today

You do not need to spend a lot of money to get more customers. You just need to help Google find you.

Go to your computer today. Check your Google map page, fix your address, and ask your next customer for a review. You will see more neighbors walking through your doors soon!
